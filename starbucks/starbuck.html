A Strawberry Mocha is a delightful fusion of rich, velvety chocolate, the boldness of espresso, and the sweet, fruity essence of strawberries. Doppio espresso, a new product

$ 20

A Doppio Espresso at Starbucks is a pure, intense coffee experience, perfect for espresso lovers. "Doppio" means "double" in Italian, so this drink features two shots of Starbucks' rich, bold espresso. The espresso is brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans under high pressure, resulting in a concentrated, smooth, and slightly bitter flavor profile with a thick, golden crema on top. The doppio offers a robust caffeine kick and a pure coffee taste with hints of caramel, nuts, and chocolate, making it an excellent choice for those who appreciate strong, unadulterated coffee.

Matcha latte macchiato, a new product

$ 20

A **Matcha Latte Macchiato** is a sophisticated blend that marries the earthy, slightly sweet flavor of matcha green tea with the creamy richness of steamed milk. In this drink, finely powdered, high-quality matcha is whisked into a small amount of water, creating a vibrant green base. This is then layered with smooth, velvety milk, and topped with a subtle foam. The result is a visually striking drink with distinct layers, where the matcha floats on top of the milk, creating a balanced flavor that is both energizing and calming. Perfect for tea lovers who enjoy a creamy, indulgent twist on traditional matcha.
